# Economics Analysis

## What Is a Converted Lead Worth?

Credex sells discounted AI infrastructure credits to startups.

The average startup using multiple AI products may spend:
- $500–$5,000/month on AI tooling
- $6,000–$60,000/year

Assuming Credex captures:
- a 10–20% margin on infrastructure resale
- or receives referral economics from providers

a realistic converted customer value could range from:
- $1,000–$8,000 annually

For modeling purposes, this analysis assumes:

- Average annual customer value: $3,000
- Average customer lifetime: 18 months
- Estimated LTV: $4,500

---

## Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC)

### Product Hunt

Estimated:
- $0 direct spend
- High founder/operator concentration

Main costs:
- preparation time
- launch coordination
- support

Effective CAC:
- approximately $10–$30 per qualified lead

---

### Twitter/X Organic Distribution

Costs:
- founder time
- content creation
- engagement

Expected CAC:
- $20–$50 per qualified lead

High variance depending on virality.

---

### Founder Outreach

Manual outreach scales poorly but creates high-quality leads.

Expected:
- 10–20% response rate
- highly qualified conversations

Estimated CAC:
- $50–$100 per qualified lead

---

## Funnel Conversion Assumptions

### Audit Completed → Lead Captured

Estimated:
- 10–20%

This is realistic because:
- value is shown before gating
- users already understand their savings potential

---

### Lead → Consultation Booked

Estimated:
- 15–25%

Users discovering:
- large overspending
- duplicated subscriptions
- enterprise inefficiencies

are strong candidates for optimization conversations.

---

### Consultation → Paid Customer

Estimated:
- 20–35%

High-savings companies are likely to convert because:
- ROI is immediately measurable
- switching costs are relatively low
- savings are recurring

## What Would Need To Be True To Reach $1M ARR?

To exceed $1M ARR within 18 months:

- strong Product Hunt or Twitter virality
- highly shareable audit pages
- strong SEO from public reports
- repeat founder referrals
- increasing AI infrastructure costs industry-wide
- efficient onboarding for high-value leads

The largest growth lever is likely:
> viral distribution through public audit reports.

Every shared report acts as:
- social proof
- acquisition content
- searchable landing page

---

## Key Economic Insight

This business works because:
- acquisition cost is relatively low
- AI tooling spend is rapidly increasing
- savings are measurable immediately
- startup founders are highly cost-sensitive

The tool itself functions as both:
- a lead-generation system
- and a trust-building mechanism.
