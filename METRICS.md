# Metrics Strategy

## North Star Metric

### Qualified Savings Opportunities Generated

Definition:
> The number of completed audits identifying meaningful monthly AI cost savings opportunities.

This metric was selected because it directly measures:
- user value creation
- lead quality
- commercial potential
- optimization relevance

The goal of the product is not daily engagement.

The goal is:
> helping startups discover financially meaningful AI overspending.

A completed audit with actionable savings is the strongest indicator that the product is creating real value.

---

## Input Metrics

### 1. Audit Completion Rate

Definition:
> Percentage of visitors who complete the audit form.

This measures:
- landing page clarity
- onboarding friction
- user intent quality
- product positioning effectiveness

A healthy target range:
- 20–35%

---

### 2. Lead Capture Rate

Definition:
> Percentage of completed audits resulting in saved reports or email submissions.

This measures:
- perceived audit value
- trust
- quality of recommendations
- conversion effectiveness

Target range:
- 10–20%

---

### 3. Share Rate of Public Reports

Definition:
> Percentage of audits resulting in shared public report URLs.

This measures:
- virality
- social proof
- emotional impact of savings discoveries
- organic distribution potential

Target range:
- 5–10%

---

### Cost Per Qualified Lead

Measures:

---

## First Instrumentation Priorities

The first analytics events I would instrument:

- landing_page_view
- audit_started
- audit_completed
- report_shared
- lead_submitted
- consultation_clicked

These events provide visibility into:
- funnel dropoff
- conversion bottlenecks
- viral mechanics
- lead quality

---

## Pivot Threshold

The main pivot signal would be:

> high audit completion but weak lead conversion.

Example:
- audit completion >25%
- lead conversion <5%

This would suggest:
- users enjoy the tool
- but do not perceive enough business value

Possible responses:
- improve recommendation quality
- improve trust signals
- improve savings explanations
- reposition toward benchmarking rather than cost reduction

---

## Key Product Insight

This product behaves more like:
- a lead-generation engine
- a financial optimization calculator

than a traditional daily-use SaaS application.

Success is therefore measured by:
- qualified opportunities
- trust generation
- and conversion efficiency

rather than recurring daily engagement.
