# Assumptions Used in Audit Logic

## Team Plan Optimization

Small teams (1–2 users) are frequently overprovisioned on collaboration-heavy plans. The audit engine recommends lower-cost alternatives when advanced administrative functionality is unlikely to be necessary.

## Enterprise Plan Optimization

Enterprise pricing generally becomes cost-effective only at larger organizational scale due to administrative overhead and bundled support costs.

## AI Workflow Optimization

Coding-heavy workflows often benefit from hybrid tooling strategies rather than concentrating spend entirely on a single provider.
